# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Students, parents, teachers, and administrators using an Arabic-first tutoring marketplace in Egypt. Visitors browse publicly; students and parents book and pay; teachers manage profile, availability, lessons, and earnings; admins verify teachers, payments, complaints, and operational state.

## Product Purpose

Help Egyptian families find trusted teachers quickly, complete bookings with manual local payment verification, and manage lessons, attendance, messaging, disputes, and payouts in one web app.

## Brand Personality

Confident, trustworthy, organized.

## Anti-references

Generic blue SaaS dashboards, playful ed-tech branding, decorative motion, and anything that obscures trust, pricing, approval, or booking status.

## Design Principles

- Trust cues must be visible before conversion.
- Public discovery should stay low-friction and approval-driven.
- Shared UI vocabulary should stay consistent across roles and surfaces.
- State changes should be explicit and easy to verify.
- Arabic-first readability and RTL flow come before ornament.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Support reduced motion, high-contrast readable text, keyboard navigation, clear focus indicators, and Arabic RTL layouts.
